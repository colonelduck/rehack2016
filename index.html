Description

A Biological product contains enzymes. These are highly efficient stain removal ingredients that work at low temperatures. Colour products do not contain bleach and so help prevent colours from fading.

Ingredients

Persil Colour Biological Tablets contain: 5-15%: Anionic Surfactants, Zeolites, <5%: Anionic Surfactants, Enzymes, Perfume , Phosphonates, Polycarboxylates

Safety

Keep away from children.
Keep away from eyes. If product gets into eyes rinse thoroughly with water.
Rinse hands after use.
